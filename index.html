Battleship

How to Play:

1. Take Turns Attacking Players: Take turns selecting a grid cell to "fire" at the opponent's board. Click a cell to attack it.

2. Hits and Misses: If you hit a ship, it's marked on the grid with a If you miss, it's marked with an

3. Bonus Turn on Hit: If you hit a ship, you get to take another turn immediately.

4. Sinking Ships A ship is sunk when all of its parts are hit. Keep track of your hits!

5. Winning the Game: The first player to sink all of the opponent’s ships wins the game.
